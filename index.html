Hello, I'm an incoming PhD student at Simon Fraser University advised by Angelica Lim and Jason Peng. I did my undergrad at Brown where I worked with Daniel Ritchie in the Visual Computing Group. I've also worked on 3D reconstruction at Vision Systems and interpretability at the Bau Lab.

I work on modeling human motion. I'm particularly interested in the affective aspects of this problem. How do we design virtual characters and humanoid robots that move in a lifelike manner? Can we get them to exhibit behaviors which accurately reflect the emotional state of the surrounding environment and people in it?
